Our Campus Story

Cartify is a campus-based snack delivery service operated by 10 dedicated students within Universiti Utara Malaysia (UUM). We know the struggle of late-night hunger when you're rushing assignments or studying for exams.


Our mission is simple: provide affordable, ready-to-eat snack bundles and deliver them fast, ensuring you never have to study on an empty stomach. From light cravings to full study-break meals, we've got your back.
